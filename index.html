About Me

A forward-thinking technology executive with a proven track record of driving enterprise-wide digital transformation, cloud adoption, and modernization across industries, including healthcare, automotive, retail, utilities, insurance, and financial services. With over 25 years of experience in IT leadership, I have consistently driven top-line growth by 15% year-over-year, reduced technology expenditures by 35% through strategic optimization, and increased operating margins by 10% with scalable and secure solutions.

As the Chief Digital and Data Officer at Cogent IBS, I lead digital innovation and cloud-first strategies for Fortune 500 clients, enabling operational efficiency, elevating customer experiences, and unlocking new revenue streams. I bring a unique ability to translate complex technology into business value, bridging the gap between IT and strategy to enable measurable outcomes.

Previously, I served as the Chief Architect at Cognizant Technologies, managing a $700M portfolio across the NAFTA region. I led a diverse team of over 200 professionals, spearheading high-impact IT service delivery and driving large-scale modernization programs, often in collaboration with CIOs, CTOs, and other C-suite stakeholders.

My expertise spans several key areas:

  • Digital Transformation: Orchestrating enterprise shifts from legacy ecosystems to agile, digital-first platforms.
  • AI/ML Integration: Embedding intelligence into business operations to enhance decision-making and service automation.
  • Cloud Strategy & Engineering: Leading end-to-end migrations to AWS and Azure, building scalable, secure cloud-native infrastructures.
  • Agile & DevSecOps Enablement: Championing agile culture and CI/CD pipelines to accelerate delivery velocity and reduce technical debt.
  • Enterprise Architecture & Microservices: Modernizing monolithic systems with modular design and microservices for improved flexibility and integration.

Earlier in my career, I held leadership roles at V2Soft, CGI, and TCS, delivering mission-critical solutions in supply chain, e-commerce, and insurance. From implementing blockchain and IoT to enhancing cybersecurity posture and uncovering vehicle vulnerabilities, I’ve consistently driven innovation that safeguards operations and elevates business value.

What sets me apart is my ability to simplify complex technology for business leaders and serve as a trusted advisor who bridges strategy with execution. My core strength is navigating organizations through change, delivering outcomes that align with business priorities, and creating long-term value.
